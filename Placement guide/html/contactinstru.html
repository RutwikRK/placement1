CInstrumentation Technology is a branch of engineering that deals with measurement and control of pressure, humidity, temperature, force, flow, pH and speed. It incorporates the concepts of control system, networking system, circuits, maintenance, protocols and basic electrical theory. There are different courses under Instrumentation Technology that educate installation process, instrumentation principle, maintenance, skills to calibrate highly complex device, testing and operation of equipment.

Instrumentation Technology: Job Roles and Salary The job roles offered to an Instrumentation Technology degree holder are highly-paying. A fresher candidate possessing the degree is offered annual salary package ranging from 2.5 lakh to 6 lakh whereas candidates with experience of more than 3 to 4 years can earn from 5 lakh to 20 lakh per annum.
